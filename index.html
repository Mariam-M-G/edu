Our mission

Give every student a fair chance to succeed at scale.

EduPulse is a web-based early warning ecosystem that unifies attendance, quiz scores, assignments, and exams into intelligent dashboards. Faculty spot struggling students weeks before finals, supervisors trigger proactive support, and top performers unlock matched internships.
